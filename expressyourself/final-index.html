Explore the World, Explore Ourselves
With the development of technology and society, people generally start to rely on mapping software such as Google Map. When people search for interesting places nearby, they usually come up with places that are commercial or famous. This makes us miss out on so many amazing and little-known beauties. So why not take action, draw your own map with your eyes and feet, and explore the unknown beauty of the world?
